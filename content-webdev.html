Search Engine Optimization

The examples below explore fairly advanced concepts of SEO. These are quantitative analyses on ranking in Google that go beyond simple checklists of using title and header tags effectively.

  • Doctor650.com Case Study - I joined The Keyword Academy (TKA) in 2010 and was mentored by Court Tuttle. Doctor650 is a case study that Court completed, post Panda and Penguin updates, to show us that it was still possible to rank websites for competitive keywords with properly designed back-links. He ranked a brand new site (doctor650.com) for the keyword '650 credit score', a very competitive keyword. Within less than a month and only 14 back-links, he was able to rank the page in the second position for this keyword. After presenting a webinar on his case study, I went through the back links and created this spreadsheet detailing the back-link profile and aggressiveness with anchor text. I also created this visual map of the link structure between webpages. This case study is the basis on which I create all SEO and content-strategy advertising for my clients.

  • SanJuanSufficiency.com Case Study - This is a case study I created for TKA, as part of its 'mastermind' group. This was created after the Doctor650 case study and it was an attempt to apply Court's linking method to two pages on my personal blog. This was a much smaller version of Court's case study applied to single pages within a site as opposed to a whole site used in the Doctor650 case study. The results were impressive. Following the same tiered linking structure as Court, I was able to get the two pages in my case study on the first page of Google for their target keywords with just a handful of links and content.

  • Keyword Analysis - The first step in assessing a site for SEO improvement is to perform a keyword analysis. Here is an example keyword analysis done on my site San Juan Sufficiency. The analysis is performed by following the instructions on this web site. The scope of the analysis is to assess what keywords a site is ranking for and to review the traffic statics on a per-keyword basis. After the keyword analysis, a navigation analysis is done. For example, in the case of WellFuture, the site was made mobile-friendly and the number of clicks required to make a purchase was reduced. The results of the keyword analysis is then used to craft an effective AdWords and Content/SEO marketing campaign.
